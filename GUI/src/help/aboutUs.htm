About Us
The developers of KodeLlama are a group of Rodman Scholars at the University of Virginia. We are all programmers to some extent and started off just like you, with a computer and no idea how to program. We thought that we could provide a tool for teaching kids like you how to program, a skill that is becoming more and more valuable in today's computer-run world. The target audience of KodeLlama is kids age 7-13, but anybody is welcome to give it a shot!

We hope you enjoy your time using KodeLlama and learn to love programming like we do!
